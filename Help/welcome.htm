Welcome to TTDX Editor Help

Thank you for downloading TTDX Editor! TTDX Editor lets you modify many features of Transport Tycoon Deluxe saved games, including:

  • Player information: money, debt, headquarters
  • Terrain: remove trees, own the roads of the AI companies, own the city bridges
  • Cities: alter company ratings with cities
  • Industries: alter production rates, cargo acceptance and production, and more
  • Stations: alter waiting cargo, ratings, etc
  • Vehicles: alter age, value, reliability, cargo
  • And more!
